Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to expand my business and increasing my number of clients. I need this website to reach more customers and expand my area of potential customers. The main purpose of this website is to show and provide a unique experience with our customers, showing them that here is the better manner to have fun, bringing a full exciting experience.

Audience

My target customers are people interested in rafting activities, either individual or as a family. Their life situacion are customers with an age range of 13-40 years old, having enough money to pay to this exciting experience.
